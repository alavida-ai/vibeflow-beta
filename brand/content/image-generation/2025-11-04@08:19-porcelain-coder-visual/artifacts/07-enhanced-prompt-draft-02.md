**Decision:** Yellow-green CRT monitor glow with yellow code highlights

**Rationale:**
- Historically authentic (vintage monitors used yellow-green phosphor)
- Maintains retro aesthetic while introducing brand color
- High visibility and impact
- Doesn't compromise code readability (just enhances it)
- Creates natural motivation for warm yellow lighting throughout scene

**Implementation in Prompt:**

**Decision:** Hybrid approach - desk accessories with personality + warmer emotional tone

**Rationale:**
- Playful elements (plant, smiling mug) add humanization
- Don't compromise seriousness of coding work
- "Approachably expert" tone requires both focus AND approachability
- Subtle personality touches feel authentic, not cartoony

**Implementation in Prompt:**

The overall mood balances focused professional work with warmth and approachability - making coding look enjoyable and human-centered, not just intense and serious"

**Expected Brand Impact:** Visual tone 20% → 65-70%, playfulness requirement met

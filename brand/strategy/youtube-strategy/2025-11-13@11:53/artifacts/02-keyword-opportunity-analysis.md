## Success Metrics for Keyword Strategy

### Primary KPIs
1. **Organic Search Traffic:** % of views from YouTube search (target: 40%+ for tutorial content)
2. **Average View Duration:** Indicator of content quality and keyword-match (target: 50%+ avg watch time)
3. **Click-Through Rate:** From search results (target: 8-12% CTR)
4. **Subscriber Conversion:** Search viewers converting to subscribers (target: 5-8%)

### Secondary KPIs
1. **Ranking Position:** Track ranking for target keywords (target: Top 3 within 3 months)
2. **Suggested Videos:** % of views from YouTube suggestions (indicates content resonance)
3. **Comments & Engagement:** Questions about specific keywords in comments
4. **External Traffic:** Shares to Reddit, Twitter, LinkedIn (indicates audience fit)

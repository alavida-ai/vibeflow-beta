## Market Demand Contradictions

### [CONTRADICTION] Claude global market share: 21% vs 3.5%
**Evidence A:** "Claude now holds **21%** of the global LLM usage market" - SQ Magazine
**Evidence B:** "Claude at 3.5%" of U.S. chatbot market - SQ Magazine

**Analysis:** Different measurement methodologies:
- 21% = Global usage across all deployment types (API, enterprise, consumer)
- 3.5% = U.S. consumer chatbot market only
- Conclusion: Claude stronger in enterprise/API use cases than consumer chatbot usage

**Strategic Implication:** Target professional/enterprise marketing audience, not casual users

### [CONTRADICTION] Claude monthly active users: 18.9M vs 30M
**Evidence A:** "18.9 million monthly active users worldwide" - Backlinko (January 2025)
**Evidence B:** "30 million monthly active users globally in Q2 2025" - SQ Magazine

**Analysis:** Possible explanations:
1. Different time periods (January vs Q2)
2. Different counting methods (website/app only vs all platforms)
3. Claude 3.5 specific (30M) vs all Claude versions (18.9M)

**Conclusion:** Use conservative 18.9M figure for TAM calculations to avoid overestimation

- Does it tell a compelling brand story?
- Can we explain why this name authentically fits?

#### Exceptional Narrative (9-10/10)

**FORJA (10/10) - The Master Craftsperson's Workshop**
- **Story:** "Forja" means forge—where master craftspeople shape raw materials through heat, force, and skill into precision tools. Just as a blacksmith forges steel into swords, you forge marketing systems through craft and expertise. Anti-AI-slop: The forge represents quality through human mastery, not volume through automation.
- **Category Fit:** Reinforces "Marketing Architecture" as craft discipline
- **Emotional Depth:** Rich craft traditions, artisan heritage, human expertise over machine replacement

- **Story:** "Tectonic" comes from Greek "tekton"—builder, architect, craftsman. When we say "tectonic shift," we mean forces that reshape entire landscapes. You're not using marketing software; you're becoming the tectonic force that reshapes your marketing infrastructure. Etymology = architect identity.
- **Category Fit:** Perfect etymological link to Marketing Architect transformation
- **Emotional Depth:** Transformative power, geological permanence, foundational systems

- **Story:** Marketing today requires orchestrating multiple agents, channels, and workflows. Like a symphony conductor, you don't play every instrument—you coordinate the ensemble. Orchestrix is your orchestration system, where AI agents are your instruments and human judgment is the conductor.

- **Emotional Depth:** Musical sophistication, coordination mastery, human leadership

**CIMIENTO (9/10) - Own the Foundation**

- **Emotional Depth:** Permanence, ownership, independence, control

**TECTARA (9/10) - The Architect's Heritage**

Like Carrara marble (the finest building material), Tectara represents precision craftsmanship in marketing architecture. You're not a marketing tactician; you're a Marketing Architect building enduring systems.
- **Category Fit:** Deepest architectural heritage, premium craft positioning
- **Emotional Depth:** Classical sophistication, timeless craft, refined quality

**ARCOVA (9/10) - The Elegant Arc**
- **Story:** An arc represents both structural engineering (Roman arches, Gothic vaults) and progressive trajectory (story arc, growth arc). Arcova combines architectural precision with innovative emergence—elegant systems design that propels you forward. Build marketing architecture with the timeless elegance of an arch.
- **Category Fit:** Architectural elegance + innovation/growth
- **Emotional Depth:** Timeless engineering, graceful strength, forward momentum

**SUBSTRATE (9/10) - The Foundational Layer**
- **Story:** In materials science, the substrate is the foundational layer upon which everything else is built. In marketing, most tools operate at the surface level—content, automation, analytics. Substrate operates at the infrastructure layer, giving you control of the foundation itself. Own the substrate, own the stack.

- **Emotional Depth:** Deep control, technical sophistication, foundational ownership

**Phase 1: US Filing (Weeks 1-2)**
- File USPTO trademark application
- Classes: 35 (marketing services) + 42 (software services)

**Phase 2: International Protection (Months 3-6)**
- Madrid Protocol for international registration
- Priority countries: Spain, Mexico, UK, Canada, Australia

**Phase 3: Defensive Registration (Year 1)**
- Register common misspellings and variations
- Secure relevant gTLDs (.io, .co, .tech)

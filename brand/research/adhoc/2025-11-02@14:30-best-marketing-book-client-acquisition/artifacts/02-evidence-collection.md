## Book 1: They Ask, You Answer by Marcus Sheridan

### Core Methodology

**The Big 5 Content Framework:**
1. **Pricing & Cost** - Answer pricing questions transparently
2. **Problems** - Address product/service limitations honestly
3. **Comparisons** - Compare yourself to competitors objectively
4. **Reviews** - Show customer reviews and results
5. **Best in Class** - Explain what makes something the best

## Book 4: Traction by Gabriel Weinberg & Justin Mares

### Core Methodology

**Bullseye Framework:**
1. Brainstorm all 19 traction channels
2. Rank channels (Outer Ring, Middle Ring, Inner Ring)
3. Prioritize tests for middle ring channels
4. Run cheap tests
5. Focus on what works

**19 Traction Channels:**
- Targeting blogs, PR, unconventional PR, search engine marketing, social/display ads, offline ads, SEO, content marketing, email marketing, viral marketing, engineering as marketing, business development, sales, affiliate programs, existing platforms, trade shows, offline events, speaking engagements, community building

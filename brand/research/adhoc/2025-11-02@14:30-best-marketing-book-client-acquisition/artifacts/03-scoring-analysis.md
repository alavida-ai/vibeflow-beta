# Scoring Analysis: Weighted Criteria Matrix

**Research Date:** 2025-11-02
**Scoring Method:** 1-10 scale per criterion, multiplied by weight

---

## Scoring Criteria (Weighted)

1. **Relevance to B2B client acquisition** (30%) - Must directly address landing enterprise/B2B clients
2. **Actionability** (25%) - Practical frameworks, not abstract theory
3. **Community/content focus** (20%) - Covers social media, community building, content strategy
4. **Stage appropriateness** (15%) - Suitable for first marketing campaign (not scaled operations)
5. **Time-to-value** (10%) - Can be read and applied quickly

**Maximum Possible Score:** 10.0

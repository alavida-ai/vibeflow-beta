# Packaging Checklist: Pre-Publication Quality Assurance

## Thumbnail Checklist

- [ ] Passes glance test (readable at 100x100 pixels)
- [ ] High contrast colors
- [ ] 3 focus areas maximum
- [ ] Central subject clear
- [ ] Text readable at small size
- [ ] Tested against similar videos in niche
- [ ] Exaggerated for appeal (not misleading)

## Title Checklist

- [ ] Under 60 characters (preferably 50)
- [ ] Human-first language (not algorithm keywords)
- [ ] Curiosity gap present
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Universal language (no unexplained jargon)
- [ ] 1-2 power words maximum
- [ ] No punctuation overuse

## Hook (First 30 Seconds) Checklist

**First 7 Seconds:**
- [ ] Confirms packaging promise (shows what thumbnail promised)
- [ ] Uses same visual framing as thumbnail
- [ ] Immediately states topic or shows payoff setup
- [ ] Viewer feels they made right click decision

**Next 23 Seconds:**
- [ ] Introduces new curiosity loop
- [ ] Pattern interrupt (visual, audio, or music shift)
- [ ] "But wait, there's more..." teaser
- [ ] Reason to keep watching established

## Synergy Checklist

- [ ] Thumbnail and title tell different parts of story (not repeat)
- [ ] Together they create compelling curiosity gap
- [ ] First 7 seconds aligns with both thumbnail and title
- [ ] Body delivers on combined promise
- [ ] Payoff satisfies expectation

## Final Pre-Publish

- [ ] Packaging promise aligns with actual content
- [ ] No retention killers identified
- [ ] 80% audience overlap with channel coherence
- [ ] Elimination criteria passed (feasible, exciting, good packaging, realistic view target)

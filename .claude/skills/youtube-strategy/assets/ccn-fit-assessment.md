# CCN Fit Assessment: Evaluating Core, Casual, New Viewer Appeal

## Rating Scale: 1-10
1-3: Very low appeal to this segment
4-6: Moderate appeal
7-10: Strong appeal

## Core Viewer Appeal: __/10
**Question:** How interested will your existing fans be?
- Are they the primary target?
- Does it satisfy their expectations?
- Will they binge multiple videos like this?

**Interpretation:**
- 8-10: They're the target audience ✅
- 5-7: Some appeal but not primary
- <5: They'll skip this video (⚠️ potential problem)

## Casual Viewer Appeal: __/10
**Question:** Do occasional viewers find value?
- Does it work without deep niche knowledge?
- Understandable to someone not familiar with niche?
- Interesting even if not a superfan?

**Interpretation:**
- 7-10: Broad appeal within niche ✅
- 5-7: Some appeal, requires context
- <5: Too niche-specific (limits growth)

## New Viewer Appeal: __/10
**Question:** Would a total stranger click this?
- Does title/thumbnail make sense without context?
- Is value clear to someone discovering you?
- Universal appeal despite niche focus?

**Interpretation:**
- 7-10: Attracts new audiences ✅
- 5-7: Some appeal, could convert some new viewers
- <5: Only existing fans click (growth limited)

## Overall Assessment

**Total Score: __/30**

- **24-30:** Excellent CCN balance. Strong growth potential. ✅ CREATE
- **18-23:** Good balance. Some growth limitation. ✅ CREATE
- **12-17:** Core-heavy, limited growth. Consider adjusting title/positioning. ⚠️ MODIFY
- **<12:** Too niche. Minimal growth potential. ❌ SCRAP

## Growth Strategy Based on Scores

### If Core > Casual + New
**Problem:** Too niche-specific
**Solution:** Reframe title for broader appeal while keeping content depth

### If all scores high (8+)
**Advantage:** Maximum growth potential
**Recommendation:** Full go-ahead

### If New < 5
**Problem:** Won't attract new audience
**Solution:** Expand title/packaging to broader appeal
**Example:** Change "D1 athlete" to "NFL players" (broader)

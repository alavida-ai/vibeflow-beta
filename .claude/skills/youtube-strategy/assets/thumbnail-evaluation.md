# Thumbnail Evaluation: Design Assessment Worksheet

## Glance Test

**Task:** Shrink thumbnail to 100x100 pixels. Can you instantly understand it?

- [ ] Yes, immediately clear
- [ ] Somewhat, takes 2-3 seconds to understand  
- [ ] No, requires thought to understand

**Result:** If not immediately clear, redesign.

## Color Contrast Assessment

**Rate 1-5 (5 = high contrast):**
- [ ] Foreground vs background contrast: __/5
- [ ] Overall "pops" against feed: __/5

**If <3:** Increase contrast, choose colors further apart.

## Text Readability

- [ ] Readable at thumbnail size?
- [ ] Font simple and clear?
- [ ] Size appropriate?
- [ ] High contrast with background?

**If NO to any:** Redesign text.

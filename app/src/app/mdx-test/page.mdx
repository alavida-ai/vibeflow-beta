## Why This Works

All components come from the design system at `/brand/strategy/design/`:

- **Design tokens** ensure brand consistency (colors, spacing, typography)
- **Components** are imported automatically via `mdx-components.tsx`
- **No manual imports needed** in MDX files

This enables AI agents to generate on-brand content by:

1. Loading design system docs: `/brand/strategy/design/DESIGN.md`
2. Loading component API: `/brand/strategy/design/components/README.md`
3. Generating MDX with these components

Result: **Brand-consistent content at scale** without manual styling.
